Our Story

Simple, affordable and fast food.

Blue Collar was founded in July 2012 in Williamsburg by local restaurateurs Gavin Compton and Jeff Slagg. Their vision was to offer simple, affordable, West Coast-style fast-food burgers reminiscent of the beloved In-N-Out chain. Compton and Slagg, known for other North Brooklyn establishments like Miller's Tavern, aimed to counter the prevalent "artisanal revolution" with a straightforward, no-frills fast-food experience. The original menu was notably simple, focusing primarily on burgers, hot dogs, fries, and milkshakes.
